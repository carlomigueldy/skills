# Intake phase

Interview one material question at a time. Record founder goals, runway,
capabilities, constraints, decision authority, and explicit assumptions in
`docs/company/` and `docs/product/`. Offer both participation modes and end at
an approval gate per `../../references/workflow.md`; apply consequential-action
limits from `../../references/policies.md`.

## Required inputs

Founder goals, constraints, authority, and any prior approved context.

## Deterministic outputs

`docs/company/intake.md` and a decision record with labeled assumptions.

## Completion criteria

Every material intake answer is recorded and the founder approves the summary.

## Invalidation rules

Changed goals, constraints, or authority make downstream strategy artifacts stale.

## Participation gate

At the start of every phase, selected independently at the start of every phase: `Interview me` or `Decide for me`; never inherit a previous selection. For `Decide for me`, record rationale, evidence, assumptions, confidence, risks, and revisit conditions.

## Approval gate

Offer approve, request changes, or go back; only approval unlocks research.
