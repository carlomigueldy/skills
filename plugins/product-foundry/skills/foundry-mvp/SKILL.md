# MVP phase

Separate the core hypothesis and testable MVP from conditional roadmap ideas.
Specify exclusions, riskiest assumptions, validation method, journey edge
states, success thresholds, and experiment duration in `docs/product/`. Do not
add speculative scope; record approval with `../../references/workflow.md`.

## Required inputs

Approved company strategy, evidence, and customer problem hypothesis.

## Deterministic outputs

`docs/product/mvp.md` with scope, exclusions, journeys, experiments, and thresholds.

## Completion criteria

The smallest testable scope and explicit exclusions are approved.

## Invalidation rules

Changed hypothesis, PMF signal, or exclusion stales prototypes, PRD, and plans.

## Participation gate

At the start of every phase, selected independently at the start of every phase: `Interview me` or `Decide for me`; never inherit a previous selection. For `Decide for me`, record rationale, evidence, assumptions, confidence, risks, and revisit conditions.

## Approval gate

Offer approve, request changes, or go back; only approval unlocks low fidelity.
