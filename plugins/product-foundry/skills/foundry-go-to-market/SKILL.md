# Go-to-market phase

Create a selective executable plan in `docs/marketing/`: positioning, category,
messaging, pricing, channel choice, landing copy, lifecycle email, social,
content/SEO, partnerships/outbound/paid decisions, launch calendar, attribution,
experiments, budget, and reusable campaign briefs. Keep every channel matched
to founder capacity and use `../../references/workflow.md` for approval.

## Required inputs

Approved company strategy, MVP, brand direction, and founder constraints.

## Deterministic outputs

`docs/marketing/go-to-market.md` with selected channels, assets, attribution, and experiments.

## Completion criteria

Every chosen channel is executable within capacity and linked to measurable learning.

## Invalidation rules

Changed ICP, pricing, thesis, or launch constraint stales messaging and launch plans.

## Participation gate

At the start of every phase, selected independently at the start of every phase: `Interview me` or `Decide for me`; never inherit a previous selection. For `Decide for me`, record rationale, evidence, assumptions, confidence, risks, and revisit conditions.

## Approval gate

Offer approve, request changes, or go back; only approval unlocks readiness.
